Project Description

This Project is about helping farmers. Traditionally farmers relied on their own knowledge to profit suitably from their farms by developing a chat-bot for agriculture we can provide great help to them. Agri-bot can assist farmers about the ideal crops which they should grow in their field, about the different disease which can affect their crop and the ways by which they can tackle these issues. Initially, we are developing an android application which can be used for disease detection and weed detection in crops. After completion of the previous step, we will focus on soil detection.The agri-bot will have data about different soil and the ideal crops which are suitable for it. The farmers will get assistance about fertilizers which can be used and the amount of water which is ideal for their crop. This will help farmers in decreasing the effective cost of growing their crop. This will also prove beneficial for the environment.

Current Progress

Currently We are Collecting the datasets of disease-stricken leaves 2) List down types of leaves and diseases associated with it, the characteristics of a disease on a certain leaf species which can help us spot it.

Project Description

For Health Care bot our main aim is to solve some of the residents' health-related issues at the home itself and also to take actions to encourage them to live a healthy lifestyle. We are using the power of machine learning and NLP to make our bot as real as possible and we are collecting a vast variety of data so that it can handle any type of situation. Whether it is an emergency or it is a fun chat. We are also focusing our bot to work for the community as by adding little-little features which can improve the lifestyle of general people greatly like whether you are drinking the proper amount of water daily and other things like diet etc.

We are exploring the ideas, any suggestions are appreciated.

Current Progress

Currently we are scraping data from the web regarding disease and health information and cleaning the data for the same.
